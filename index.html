Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025

At NutriSnap (the "App"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our app, which is a Trusted Web Activity (TWA) that saves data locally in your browser and uses Gemini for photo analysis.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

The app saves data locally in your browser using browser storage technologies, such as localStorage or IndexedDB. This data includes user preferences, inputs, and app usage history, and is not sent to external servers.

Photo Analysis Data

If you use the photo analysis feature, Gemini processes the photos you upload to analyze their content. We do not store the photos you upload permanently, but Gemini may temporarily store them for processing purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Local Data

The data stored locally in your browser is used to improve your experience within the app, such as remembering preferences and inputs between sessions. This data is not shared or sent to external servers.

Photo Analysis

If you upload a photo for analysis, Gemini processes the photo and returns analysis results. The data may include metadata related to the photo (e.g., dimensions, content features, etc.). However, we do not store or retain the uploaded photo permanently, and it is used solely for analysis within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Gemini

We use Gemini to perform photo analysis within the app. Gemini may collect and temporarily store the photos you upload for processing purposes. You can review Gemini's privacy policy for more details about their data practices.

4. Data Security

The data stored locally on your browser is protected by the security measures provided by your browser. However, since Gemini processes your photos, we encourage you to review Gemini's privacy practices regarding how they handle the data.

If your device is shared or accessed by others, the locally stored data (including photo metadata) may be accessible. We recommend keeping your device secure to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

Local Data

Data stored locally in your browser will remain until you choose to delete it manually or clear your browser's cache and local storage.

Photo Data

The photos you upload for analysis are temporarily processed by Gemini. We do not store the photos permanently in our systems, but Gemini may retain the data according to their own retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information or uploaded a photo, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
